Lecture Notes



Here are several notes I wrote while attending various courses. Next to each file, you'll find the last modified date, a download link, and a brief description of the content.

At the moment, these notes are in Italian, and some parts are handwritten. I might revise and rewrite them in English in the future; at the same time, I would like to expand them further and possibly consolidate some of them.

Lie Algebras and Lie Groups 16/06/2021 PDF I miei appunti del corso tenuto dai professori Giovanni Gaiffi ed Enrico Le Donne nell'anno accademico 2018/2019.
Principali argomenti del corso: - Lie Algebras: definitions and first properties. Lie Algebra homomorphisms. Simple, semisimple, nilpotent and solvable Lie Algebras. Killing form. - Representation theory of Lie algebras (first part): definitions and first properties. Casimir element. Weyl Theorem. The algebra sl(2, K) and its irreducible reprentations. Standard cyclic modules. Weight lattice and examples. - Root systems: Dynkin diagrams and their complete classification. Weyl groups. Connection between Lie Algebras and root system: Lie theorem and semisimplicity criterion. Toral algebras. - Poincaré-Birkhoff-Witt theorem and Serre relations. - Lie Groups: definitions and first properties. Lie Group homomorphisms. The Lie Algebra of GL (n, R). One-parameter subgroups and subalgebras. Exponential map: from Lie groups to associated Lie algebras. Adjoint representation. Baker-Cambpell-Hausdorff formula.
Elementary Number Theory 25/06/2024 PDF I miei appunti del corso tenuto dal professor Giuseppe Puglisi nell'anno accademico 2017/2018.
Principali argomenti del corso: - The problem of the distribution of primes: the Prime Number Theorem. Lagrange's four-squares theorem. Quadratic residues. The quadratic reciprocity law. - Quadratic congruences and congruences of higher degree. The structure of multiplicative groups modulo $q.$ - Arithmetic functions. Dirichlet series and infinite products. Asymptotic behaviour of arithmetic functions. Mertens and Chebychev theorems. Dirichlet theorem on prime numbers in progressions.
Modular Forms 21/01/2022 PDF I miei appunti del corso tenuto dai professori Andrea Maffei e Davide Lombardo nell'anno accademico 2020/2021.
- Principali argomenti del corso: The modular group $\text{SL}_2(\mathbb{Z}).$ Classical modular forms, Fourier expansions. Weight of a modular form. Examples: Eisenstein series, the discriminant. Modular forms of arbitrary weight form a polynomial algebra in the variables $E_4, E_6.$ - Classical examples: theta series associated with plane lattices. Representations of integers as sums of squares. - Generalisation to congruence subgroups of $\text{SL}_2(\mathbb{Z}).$ Geometric interpretation. - Hecke operators, recurrence relations. Properties of Ramanujan's tau function. The Petersson inner product. Simultaneous eigenvectors for the Hecke operators. - $L$-functions attached to modular forms. Functional equation in the cuspidal case. Euler product decomposition of the $L$-function attached to an eigenform

Principali argomenti del corso: - Singular homology: construction and basic properties. Applications. CW-complexes, cellular homology. - Cohomology ring, cup product, Poincaré duality. - Homotopy groups: construction and basic properties. Cellular approximation. Homotopy groups of spheres.
Elements of Complex Analysis 15/02/2022 PDF I miei appunti del corso tenuto dal professor Marco Abate nell'anno accademico 2020/2021.
Principali argomenti del corso: - Complements of complex analysis in one variable: compact-open topology and topology of the uniform convergence on compact sets. Convergence of sequences of holomorphic functions; compactness in the space of holomorphic functions. - Hurwitz theorems. Schwarz lemma. Automorphisms of the unit disk, of the half-plane, of the complex plane, of the Riemann sphere. - Poincaré distance. Wolff-Denjoy theorem. Riemann uniformization theorem. Approximation of holomorphic functions; applications. Construction of global functions starting from local data. - Introduction to the complex analysis in several variables: definitions and examples. Cauchy-Riemann conditions and consequences. Analytic continuation principle. Cauchy integral formula. Cauchy inequalities. Maximum principle. Weierstrass, Montel and Vitali theorems. Hartogs extension theorem. Domains of holomorphy. Convex and pseudoconvex domains. Plurisubharmonic functions. Levi problem and its solution.
