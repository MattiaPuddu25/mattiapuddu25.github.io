Both files contain identical theory notes, intended to support those training for competitions in their preparation.

These notes collect, in a way I hope is reasonably clear and well-organized, the tools I use in the various solutions — tools that are typically not covered by standard high school curricula.

  • A - Geometry in coordinates and vectors

  • B - Power of a point with respect to a circle

  • C - Cevians

  • D - Notable points of a triangle

  • E - Quadrilaterals and inequalities between means

  • F - Summations & products

  • G - Angles that are multiples of 18 degrees

  • H - Burnside's lemma

  • I - Congruences and divisibility among integers

  • J - Complex numbers

  • K - Polynomials

  • L - Sequences and recurrence equations

  • M - Approximations and continued fractions

  • N - Diophantine equations

  • O - Binomial coefficients

  • P - Generating functions

  • Q - Partitions

  • R - Graph theory

  • S - Polyhedra

  • T - Lattices

  • U - Arithmetic functions

  • V - p-adic valuations
